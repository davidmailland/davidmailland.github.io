Publications

From Mathematics to Art: Modelling the Pascal’s Triangle with Petri Nets

Published in Symmetry (Special Issue), 2025

We propose a Petri-net-based modelling of Pascal’s triangle, bridging discrete mathematics and formal modelling. The approach supports structural insights and visually interpretable representations.

Recommended citation:
Mailland, D.; Grobelna, I. (2025). From Mathematics to Art: Modelling the Pascal's Triangle with Petri Nets. Symmetry, 17(12), 2181. DOI: 10.3390/sym17122181

A Novel Approach to the Collatz Conjecture with Petri Nets

Published in Information (MDPI), 2025

We propose a Petri-net-based modelling of the inverse dynamics of the Collatz conjecture. The construction reveals a structured tree of predecessors up to a bounded domain and allows the study of reachability, liveness, and recurrence properties using formal methods.

Recommended citation:
Mailland, D.; Grobelna, I. (2025). A Novel Approach to the Collatz Conjecture with Petri Nets. Information, 16(9), 745. DOI: 10.3390/info16090745

Design of Automotive HMI: New Challenges in Enhancing User Experience, Safety, and Security

Published in Applied Sciences (MDPI), 2025

This narrative review addresses recent developments and future challenges in the design of automotive human–machine interfaces (HMIs), with a focus on user experience, safety, and security.

Recommended citation:
Grobelna, I.; Mailland, D.; Horwat, M. (2025). Design of Automotive HMI: New Challenges in Enhancing User Experience, Safety, and Security. Applied Sciences, 15(10), 5572. DOI: 10.3390/app15105572
