This page gathers visual explorations arising from mathematical structures. The emphasis is on representations that sit at the intersection of formal modelling, discrete mathematics, and visual intuition.

Pascal’s Triangle as a Petri Net

This figure illustrates a Petri-net-based modelling of Pascal’s triangle. The construction highlights the underlying combinatorial structure while offering a visually interpretable representation of binomial relations.

The same formal model supports both analytical reasoning and graphical exploration, opening the door to visual interpretations that naturally resonate with generative or artistic perspectives.
