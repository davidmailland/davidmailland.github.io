From Mathematics to Art: Modelling the Fibonacci Sequence with Petri Nets

Published in Fractal and Fractional, 10(1), 53

We propose a Petri-net-based modelling of the Fibonacci recurrence, bridging discrete mathematics and formal modelling. The approach supports structural insights and visually interpretable representations.
